Livelihood Development

Through this program, we seek to reduce hunger and
poverty in vulnerable communities by mobilizing support for
food and financial aid.

Promote personal development and fight social vices among

Tennis Development

We seek to promote the sport of tennis to enhance physical
fitness, social interaction and professional tennis playing.
This program targets school-going children from vulnerable
households in Nairobi and Deaf youths.
